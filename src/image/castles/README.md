# Castle Images

Please place the Heroes of Might and Magic 3 castle images in this directory with the following filenames:

- `castle.png`, `castle.jpeg`, `castle.jpg`, or `castle.gif` - Castle town
- `rampart.png`, `rampart.jpeg`, `rampart.jpg`, or `rampart.gif` - Rampart town
- `tower.png`, `tower.jpeg`, `tower.jpg`, or `tower.gif` - Tower town
- `inferno.png`, `inferno.jpeg`, `inferno.jpg`, or `inferno.gif` - Inferno town
- `necropolis.png`, `necropolis.jpeg`, `necropolis.jpg`, or `necropolis.gif` - Necropolis town
- `dungeon.png`, `dungeon.jpeg`, `dungeon.jpg`, or `dungeon.gif` - Dungeon town
- `stronghold.png`, `stronghold.jpeg`, `stronghold.jpg`, or `stronghold.gif` - Stronghold town
- `fortress.png`, `fortress.jpeg`, `fortress.jpg`, or `fortress.gif` - Fortress town
- `conflux.png`, `conflux.jpeg`, `conflux.jpg`, or `conflux.gif` - Conflux town
- `cove.png`, `cove.jpeg`, `cove.jpg`, or `cove.gif` - Cove town (HotA)
- `factory.png`, `factory.jpeg`, `factory.jpg`, or `factory.gif` - Factory town (HotA)

## Image Requirements
- Recommended size: 48x48 pixels
- Format: PNG, JPEG, JPG, or GIF (all supported)
- Transparent background preferred (PNG or GIF recommended)

## How to Obtain Images Legally
1. Extract from your own legitimate copy of Heroes 3
2. Use fan-made recreations (ensure they're free to use)
3. Create your own castle icons

## Fallback
If images are not available locally, the application will fall back to external URLs from heroes.thelazy.net
